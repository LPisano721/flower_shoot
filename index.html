info About Us



We are an energetic and detail-oriented wedding planning & photography company looking to assist clients in coordinating and executing beautiful weddings. Flower Shoot's responsibilities include obtaining quotes from vendors, ensuring that budget changes are incorporated into our clients’ wedding plans accordingly, and collecting payments from clients for the services provided. We are also able to create a time-line of all wedding-day activities for each client to ensure that everything remains on schedule.

We are methodical and adept at handling difficult or stressful situations. Ultimately, as a top-notch wedding company, we keep abreast of the latest wedding trends as well as demonstrate exceptional communication, organization, and customer service skills.

people Meet Our Staff



Jorge McGreenhill has created a mock company called Flower Shoot Incorporated during the pandemic of 2020 after he noticed that people who were in love with one another had nowhere to turn to perform their weddings. Under the federal guidelines, Jorge and his wonderful company not only perform weddings, and create media of the weddings, but now also preside over them.

Meet Miranda Stillabower; she is our mock wedding coordinator and is highly regarded by our clientele as being quite empathetic to their ideas and inputs on their dream weddings. Miranda makes the magic happen; she also loves cats!

This is Roll Safe, a character created by the British filmmaker and actor Kayode Ewumi. Roll Safe is played by Reece Simpson who coincidentally is our mock photographer and videographer. When it comes to art, Reece Simpson is very provocative and makes us think.

On call, we have Leonardo DiCaprio as our mock transportation driver. he and his team of mock transporters will take you to your destination(s) safely and reliably. He even does autographs.
